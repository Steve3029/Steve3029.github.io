Motivation

4 Jan 2018

How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

Like most of people, I believed that pleasure was the source of happiness. But I was not able to find something that could bring me sustainable pleasure. At that time, I just thought maybe I had not found something that really brought me sustainable pleasure. I never realized I needed passion and higher purpose. I guess I could get my sustainable happiness by setting my higher purpose and putting passion into it.

What are your core values?

1. I make a living by creating value for others. The 'others' may be a individual, a organization, a country, or the world that we are living in. 2. Creation can change the world. It will make human life better.

What do you stand for?

I am sure that I need more time to think about it.

How did you find the resilience ideas and meditation exercise?

I was ever pretty bad at sports. So one day, I decided to begin do running exercise. After three months, I completed my first ten km. I used this as my success experience in resilience meditation exercise. And In a college entrance examination, I got a poor grades. It might affect my future studies. That time I was desperate. I used it as my failure experience in my resilience meditation exercise.
