How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

I rather consider the 'process over product' as a kind of work flow. It makes the design and build process very clear and orderly. I took the following process.

  1. develop the layout of the page.
  2. convert the wireframe into semantic html.
  3. set up the style from big elements to small elements.
  4. improve and adjust details.

What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

Before, I thought meditation was a kind mysterious practice that could only be mastered by a few monks. I also thought meditators could absorb the energy of nature to become more powerful. I thought of the meditators as a kind of people who was very close to God.

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

I have know that meditation is just a kind of mental practice that everyone can take like physical practice. Through the meditation, people will become more healthy, happiness, and focused. I have learned almost anything about how to do a meditate. If I want to meditate I whould create a intention, then follow my breath. When my attention wander away I should bring it back gently. The good way to bring my attention back is ackownledge, experience without judging and reacting, if need to react, continue maintaining mindfulness, let it go.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

I tried the meditation instruction in the chapter 2. Actually, It is very hard to me that is to open my eyes slightly. I prefer to close my eyes. And ten minute is a little long to me.
