Leadership

5 Jan 2018

What is your experience of compassionate (or not) leadership?

I used met a boss who actually was my boss's boss. He had a high professional level, but he never show off it. Whether my boss or I had any hardship at work, he always kinkly help us. He was always willing to listen our opinions whenever a decision needed to be made. He was more like a trusted friend when not working.

Do the multiplying goodness meditation. How did you find it?

Be honest, I do not think I am a compassionate man and I do not have a lot of friends either. But I am sure If i can deliver goodness to others I must be happiness. I gradually began to pay attention to my communication with other people and I could understand others more when I started to practice the goodness meditation. When others encounter difficulties i prefer to help them.

Do the Tonglen meditation. How did you find it?

I do not try the Tonglen meditation yet because I guess the Tonglen is beyond my ability. I do not think I am a compassionate people before. I started to learn how to be the one. So I am trying the multiplying goodness meditation. Maybe I will try it three weeks later.

How does the SCARF model apply to your Phase 0 experience so far? How will it apply to bootcamp?

I felt deeply the two point in the SCARF model. If the week's mission is completely new to me I will become a little anxiety, because it is uncertainty. If I run into problems, I will ask for help if I do not find the cause of the problem for more than half an hour. Most of time, I need to solve them by myself. I will be stressful because I can not get others help. I now know the working mechanism of the brain is the case.

Have a difficult conversation. How did it go?

I do not have chance to have a difficult conversation yet. I am looking forward to practice the conversation skill in the future.
